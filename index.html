The problem

Paper JMPs are a daily headache.

Every field team in Australia is dealing with the same frustrations. Here's what JMPHQ fixes.

01
✍️

You write everything from scratch. Every. Single. Time.

Driver name, mobile, next of kin, vehicle, route, checkpoints, call-in schedule — all re-entered on every form. If your team does 3 runs a week to the same site, that's 3x the same data written by hand.

JMPHQ fix Driver profiles auto-fill your details. Repeat a route and everything comes back — origin, destination, checkpoints, call-in schedule. Update the date and go.
02
📵

Once they leave, you have zero visibility.

The driver has a paper form. You have a copy on your desk. They're somewhere between Perth and Newman and you won't know if something's wrong until they don't show up.

JMPHQ fix Live dashboard shows every active journey, colour-coded by status. Missed checkpoint? SMS to the supervisor instantly. Overdue? Alert fires automatically.
03
🖊️

Chasing signatures is a full-time job.

Supervisor is offsite. Driver needs to leave in 20 minutes. Cue the phone calls, the WhatsApp messages, the "can you just sign this when you get back" — and the journey that leaves without approval.

JMPHQ fix Supervisor approves from their phone in one tap. Saved signature stamps automatically. No paper, no chasing, no exceptions.
04
📂

Audit time is a nightmare.

Forms get lost. Signatures fade. Someone filed last month's JMPs in the wrong folder. When a safety audit lands, you're scrambling through filing cabinets hoping everything's there.

JMPHQ fix Every journey, signature, checkpoint, and approval is stored permanently. PDF export on demand. Full audit trail with timestamps — ready in seconds, not hours.
How it works

Plan. Approve. Track. Complete.

Four steps that replace every paper form you've ever printed.

01
🗺️

Map your route, set checkpoints at towns along the way, add passengers, fill the pre-journey checklist, and schedule your return leg — all in one flow.

→
02
✅

Supervisor reviews and approves with a single tap. Recurring routes can be pre-approved. Everyone signs digitally — no paper, no chasing.

→

→

Features

Everything your HSE team needs.

Built around the way field teams actually work — not how a boardroom thinks they do.

📲

QR Vehicle Stickers

One permanent QR per vehicle. Scan to start, scan at checkpoints, scan to complete. The whole journey flows from a sticker on the dashboard.

🛡️

Smart Pre-Journey Checklist

All HSE checklist items digitised. Admin can toggle any item to "blocking" — a No answer stops the journey until resolved. Full compliance, every time.

✓ First aid kit present
✓ Vehicle pre-start inspection
✓ 10L water minimum
✗ Road conditions checked Blocked
✍️

Digital Signatures

Drivers and passengers sign on screen. Supervisors approve with a saved signature — stamped automatically on every journey they approve.

📡

Live Dashboard

See every active journey at a glance. Colour-coded status — on track, due for check-in, overdue. Click any journey for full detail and history.

💾

Your data stays. You don't re-enter it.

Driver profile, next of kin, mobile, licence — saved once, auto-filled every time. Repeat a route and the origin, destination, checkpoints, and call-in schedule come back pre-filled. No one on your team should be writing the same thing twice.

✓ Driver details auto-filled from profile
✓ Repeat route — same stops, same schedule
✓ Frequent routes can be pre-approved
🚨

Overdue Alerts

Miss a checkpoint? Go overdue? Your supervisor gets an SMS instantly via Twilio. Configurable per journey — some runs need monitoring, some don't.

🚗

Convoy Support

Link multiple vehicles to one journey. Each driver completes their own checklist independently. One approval covers the whole convoy.

🎨

White-Label Ready

Upload your logo, set your brand colour. The platform looks like yours. JMPHQ appears only in the footer — your team sees your brand every day.
